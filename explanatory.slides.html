ABV vs. IBU: Overall¶

We can see that ABV and IBU are highly correlated. They have a correlation of 0.73. Meaning if one increases, there is a high chance the other would increase too.

In other words, with the increase in the alcohol percentage, the bitterness increases.

ABV vs. IBU: Top 10 Beer Styles¶

I chose the top 10 Beer styles based on the count of different beers for each style. The results were pretty interesting. Previously, we saw that ABV and IBU are highly correlated but based on the type of beer, the slopes change. For example, for American Pale Wheat Ale the Bitterness would be constant regardless of the alcohol content the beer has.
